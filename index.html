Game Story

This game is a variation of the famous rock-paper-scissors that first appeared in episode 8, second season of the popular SITCOM The Big Bang Theory, when Raj and Sheldon decide to use it to decide what they will see on television. Later on the same TV show, credit would be given to the true inventors of the game, Sam Kass and Karen Bryla.

Instrucctions

In the game the computer and the player face each other revealing their choice at the same time. If both chose the same one, it is considered a draw and will not increase the score of either of them. Under this Instrucctions you will find the table that indicates the winner in each case. The best of 5 rounds wins the match.
